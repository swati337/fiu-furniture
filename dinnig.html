Dinning Table

A Dining table is a central piece of furniture that brings people together for shared meals, conversation, and bonding. It typically consists of a flat, rectangular, round, or oval surface supported by sturdy legs, often crafted from wood, metal, or a combination of both. The design of a dining table can vary from traditional to contemporary, featuring intricate carvings or sleek, minimalist lines. The size and shape of the table are important for fitting the space and accommodating the number of people who will use it. Some dining tables include extension leaves for added seating capacity, while others may feature decorative elements such as inlays, glass tops, or polished finishes.
