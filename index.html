Sofa Sets Features

    Frame Material
  • Wood: Solid hardwood frames (like oak, maple, or walnut) tend to be sturdy and long-lasting.
  • Metal: Often used in modern designs for a sleek, industrial look; tends to be very durable.
    Cushioning
  • Foam: Common for its support and comfort. Higher-density foam tends to last longer and retain its shape better.
  • Down/Feather: Soft and plush, but may require more maintenance as feathers can compress over time.
    Design & Style
  • Contemporary: Modern aesthetics with sleek lines, minimalistic design, and neutral tones.
  • Traditional: Classic styles with ornate carvings, luxurious fabrics, and intricate detailing.
    Material & Upholstery
  • Fabric: Soft, breathable, and available in a range of colors and textures (linen, velvet, cotton).
  • Leather: Durable, easy to clean, and elegant, offering a more polished, upscale look.
    Size & Configuratio
  • Loveseat: Seats 2 people, great for smaller spaces.
  • 3-Seater / 4-Seater: Ideal for larger seating areas.
  • Modular Sectionals: Can be customized to fit specific spaces.
